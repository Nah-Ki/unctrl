**UNCTRL** is not your average game. It's a *terminal-born*, retro-fueled warzone built for killers who think fast, strike hard, and never miss. You’re dropped into a neon hellscape with nothing but your instincts, your reflexes, and the weapons you earn through blood and grit. No tutorials. No mercy. Just chaos.

1v1. AI swarms. Hardcore formations. No RNG—just skill.

---

## ⚙ Tech Stack

- **Language:** C++ 
- **Terminal Graphics:** `ncurses`
- **Multiplayer:** TCP Sockets 
- **Build Tool:** `Make`
- **Platform:** Linux 
- **Editor of Choice:** Neovim
